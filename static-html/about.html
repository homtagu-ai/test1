About CarVault

Dubai's premier car buyout service with 15+ years of experience. We're revolutionizing how people sell their cars in the UAE.

Our Story

Founded in 2009, CarVault has been at the forefront of the UAE's automotive industry. What started as a small family business in Dubai has grown into the region's most trusted car buyout service, helping thousands of customers get fair prices for their vehicles.

We recognized early on that traditional car selling was broken - too many middlemen, unfair pricing, and lengthy processes. That's why we built CarVault to offer direct buyouts with transparent pricing and instant payments.

Today, we're proud to be the first car buyout service in the UAE to offer instant cryptocurrency payments, giving our customers more flexibility and better terms than ever before.
