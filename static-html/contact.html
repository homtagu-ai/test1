Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I get a car valuation?

Our online valuation tool provides instant results. For a detailed inspection-based offer, we typically respond within 2-4 hours during business hours.

Do you buy cars in any condition?

Yes! We buy cars in any condition - from brand new to heavily damaged. Our valuations are based on current market value and condition.

What documents do I need?

You'll need your Emirates ID, car registration (mulkiya), and any service records. We handle all the transfer paperwork for you.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees! Our service is completely free for sellers. The only fees are standard government transfer fees, which we'll explain upfront.
